Frequently Asked Questions

How far do you travel?
Truly Enchanted charges no fee for events and parties within a 25-mile radius. For events outside of that range there is a $25.00 travel fee.
What ages are your birthday parties appropriate for?
Our parties are most appropriate for ages 4 through 8. Children under the age of 4 are too young for our princess party activities.
Can I leave a child/children alone with the princess?
Absolutley not - we require that there is at least one parent or guardian in the room with the children for the entirety of the time that the princess is visiting.
If the children start to get out of hand during a party, can the princess or her royal maid help keep them under control?
Once in a while, in all of the excitement, a parental figure will need to step in and help with a child. If you see that one of the guests is interfering with a game or preventing the party from running smoothly, we ask that the hostess or any other adult figure take care of the matter.
Do the children need to wear costumes at a princess party or event?
The children and guests can wear regular clothing, however costumes are encouraged! Getting dressed up helps with the excitement and anticipation of the princess visit.
Can we have the party outside?
Sorry, no. We need to keep our princess costumes and wigs inside due to weather elements that may damage them. We also need an indoor outlet for our music.
Do I need to count babies and toddlers in the guest count for the party?
No - children under the age of 3 are too young for the activities.
If some of my guests don't show up, do I get a discount?
No - our parties are at a set price and for up to either 10 or 15 children (including the birthday girl) depending on the package you purchase.
Is smoking allowed near the princesses?
Absolutely not. Smoking while the princess is present will result in an immediate end to her visit with no refund.
Can my pets be around the princess?
No. Pets are not allowed nearby or around the princess in order to protect our princess costumes.
